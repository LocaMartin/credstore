Program Rules

Do not access third-party data, publish active exploit details before remediation, or attack infrastructure outside CredStore-owned surfaces. Good-faith reports are reviewed through the internal ticketing system.

In-scope targets

  • CredStore desktop app for Linux, Windows, and macOS.
  • CredStore Android APK and iOS app builds.
  • CredStore website and static endpoints under https://locamartin.github.io/credstore/*.

Out-of-scope activity

  • Denial-of-service, spam, social engineering, physical attacks, or third-party service abuse.
  • Accessing, copying, modifying, or deleting data that does not belong to your own test account/device.
  • Public disclosure before CredStore has validated and remediated the issue.

Public recognition is published on the dedicated Hall of Fame and advisory pages after validation.
