Anti-piracy model

Offline apps cannot completely prevent someone from copying a license token. CredStore reduces abuse by using signed tokens, local device limits, optional company/buyer metadata, no online activation tracking, and screenshot protection inside the desktop/mobile app where the OS supports it.
